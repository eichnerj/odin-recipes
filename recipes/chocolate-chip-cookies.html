Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 155g AP Flour
  • 75g Sugar
  • 80g Brown Sugar
  • 1 Cup Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Vanilla
  • Flaked Salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Soften Butter
  2. In a bowl, combine flour (155g), salt (1/2tsp), and baking soda (1/2tsp)
  3. In mixing bowl, combine butter (1 stick), sugar (75g), brown sugar (80g), and vanilla (1/2 tsp)
  4. Mix on 4 until creamy. Add egg, mix well. Gradually mix in flour mixture on 2. Stir in chocolate chips (1 cup)
  5. Refrigerate dough for 1 hour minimum. Remove from fridge, form into teaspoon size balls, freeze if desired.
  6. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F. Bake for 11 minutes (13 minutes if frozen)
  7. Top with flaked salt if desired.
